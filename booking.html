When you buy tickets online you will be sent an email with a reference code. If you are unable to print please make sure you bring along the unique reference and surname for the booking. You are welcome to show the code via phone or tablet also. Please allow 30 minutes for emails to arrive.

Please be aware that all children must be accompanied by an adult. Unfortunately, refunds are not available. Tickets purchased online cannot be used in conjunction with any other offer.

Please click on our 'Prices' page for our Admission Policy including documentation required for carers and guests with disabilities. Without correct documenation we will be unable to offer our 1 to 1 concession.

Tickets are valid for 1 use within 6 months of purchase. If you wish to change the date of your visit, please note that you will be charged the difference between your booked ticket and standard entry on the day.
